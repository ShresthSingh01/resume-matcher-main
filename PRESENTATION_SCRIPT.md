# Virex AI - Presentation Script (Hinglish)

**Theme:** Professional, Enthusiastic, and Story-driven.
**Target Audience:** Judges (Technical & Non-Technical).
**Time:** ~3-5 Minutes.

---

## 1. Introduction (The Hook)
**Speaker 1:**
"Good Morning Judges! Hiring process mein sabse badi problem kya hai? It's **Volume** and **Bias**."

"Aaj kal ek job post pe 500 se zyada resumes aate hain. Ek recruiter ke paas practically itna time hi nahi hai ki wo har resume ko dhang se padhe. Result? **Manual Bias** aur **Missed Opportunities**."

"Isliye aaj hum present kar rahe hain **Virex AI** - A Next-Generation **Intelligent Resume Matcher & AI Interviewer**."

---

## 2. The Problem (Pain Points)
**Speaker 1:**
"Screen pe dekhiye. Traditional process mein kya hota hai?
1.  **Recruiters drown in resumes**: Keywords search karte reh jaate hain, context miss ho jata hai.
2.  **Scheduling Nightmares**: Initial screening calls schedule karne mein weeks lag jaate hain.
3.  **Cheating Risks**: Remote interviews mein candidate actually skilled hai ya ChatGPT use kar raha hai, pata lagana mushkil hai."

"Humne in teeno problems ko ek single automated pipeline se solve kiya hai."

---

## 3. The Solution (Our Workflow)
**Speaker 2 (Technical Lead):**
"Virex AI simple keyword matching nahi karta. Ye poora hiring process automate karta hai in **3 Simple Phases**."

### Phase 1: Smart Ingestion & Parsing
"Sabse pehle, recruiter bulk mein resumes upload karta hai aur Job Description (JD) deta hai.
Humara system **LLM (Large Language Model)** use karke har resume se structured data extract karta hai—Skills, Education, Experience—chahe format kaisa bhi ho."

### Phase 2: The "Brain" (Ranking System)
"Yahan magic start hota hai. Humara logic sirf keyword count nahi karta. Humne ek **5-Point Likert Scale** banaya hai."
"System har candidate ko 5 parameters pe rate karta hai: Education, Experience, Projects, Skills, aur Certifications."

"Agar score **60% se kam** hai, toh candidate auto-reject. Recruiter ka time waste hi nahi hoga.
Jo select hue, unhe milta hai ek **Unique Interview Link**."

---

## 4. The Core Feature: AI Interviewer (The "Wow" Factor)
**Speaker 2:**
"Ab aate hain sabse cool feature pe—**The AI Interviewer**."
"Selected candidates ko kisi human ka wait nahi karna. Wo kabhi bhi link open karke apna technical round de sakte hain."

*   **Real-time Voice**: "Ye chat-bot nahi hai. Candidate bol ke answer deta hai, aur AI wapas bolta hai using **ElevenLabs** voices. Bilkul natural conversation hoti hai."
*   **Adaptive Questions**: "Agar candidate ne bola 'Mene React use kiya hai', toh AI puchhega 'React Context API ki performance implications kya hain?'. Ratta-fication nahi chalega."

---

## 5. Security & Anti-Cheating
**Speaker 1:**
"Par online interview mein cheating toh ho sakti hai? Absolutely not."
"Humne implementation kiya hai ek **Strict Anti-Cheating Protocol**:
1.  **Fullscreen Lock**: Agar candidate ne fullscreen exit kiya -> **Warning**.
2.  **Tab Switching**: Agar tab switch kiya -> **Termination**.
DO warnings ke baad session auto-terminate ho jata hai. No mercy for cheaters."

---

## 6. The Result: Actionable Insights
**Speaker 1:**
"Interview khatam hote hi, Recruiter ke dashboard pe ek **Live Leaderboard** aa jata hai."
"Score kaise banta hai?
**40% Resume Score + 60% Interview Performance.**"

"Recruiter ko detailed feedback milta hai: 'Concept clear tha, but implementation weak thi'.
Bas ek click pe—**Green Button** se 'Select' aur **Red Button** se 'Reject'."

---

## 7. Conclusion
**Speaker 1 & 2:**
"So, **Virex AI** is not just a tool; it's a **Digital Recruiter**.
It makes hiring **Faster**, **Fairer**, and **Future-ready**.
Thank you! We are open for questions."
